Welcome to CazApp

Our mission is to empower users to make the best transport decisions based on climate regulation.

We want to aid the governmentâ€™s commitment to building a cleaner, healthier environment to benefit the people of the UK and its economy. Poor air quality poses the largest environmental risk to public health in the UK, the implementation of clean air zone initiatives hope to combat this by reducing sources of fine particulate matter that predominantly stem from car, lorry, bus and other vehicle exhausts. We hope to encourage users to interact with the zone, avoiding a fine and so be able to engage with all the positive outcomes the CAZ aims to achieve. As clean air zones are being developed in regions all over the UK, the eventual expansion of this app will provide great benefits to drivers all over the country.
