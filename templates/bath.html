The Bath Clean Air Zone

Several areas within Bath city exceed legal limits of harmful particulate matter produced from vehicle exhausts. Following public consultation in 2018, the Bath Clean Air Zone (CAZ) was implemented in the city centre on 15 March 2021.

The Bath CAZ is a Type C zone and charges apply 24 hours a day, all year round. Types of vehicles currently charged include coaches and buses, trucks and lorries (HGVs), taxis and private hire vehicles, minibuses, some camper vans and four-by-fours, private heavy goods vehicles (i.e. horse transporters or motorhomes). Charges range between £9 and £100.
